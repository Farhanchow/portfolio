About Me

Who Am I

My name is Md Farhan Chowdhury. I’m a passionate Creative Web Developer and Designer, with a love for digital storytelling. I’m obsessed by usability, and constantly look to create intriguing and concept enhancing interactions that remain intuitive for the user. I’m obsessed with details, and feel strongly about the importance and benefits of prototyping during the design stage of a project. I enjoy playing creatively with technology, to see how I can invent my own custom digital experiences. I am currently majoring in Computer Science (BS-Bachelor of Science ) at Long Island University Brooklyn and expected to graduate by 2017. I specialize in CMS based environment with experience using - WordPress, Webflow, Weebly, Jimdo and Optimizing Website for mobile platforms.

I work with: HTML | CSS | Bootstrap | JavaScript

Graphic Design: Adobe Photoshop CC | SmartDraw | Logomakr Web Tool

Academic Student Experience with: SQL | Oracle DBMS | Java | C++
